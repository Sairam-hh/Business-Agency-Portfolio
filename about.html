Our Story

From a Two-Person Studio to a Global Digital Agency

NovaPixel started in a small co-working space in San Francisco with just two founders, a laptop, and a bold vision: to make premium digital design accessible to growing brands. What began as a side project quickly turned into a full-fledged agency as word spread about our quality and speed.

Over seven years, we've grown into a 40-person team serving clients across 35 countries. We've shipped over 250 projects — from startup landing pages to enterprise-grade platforms — and picked up 12 industry awards along the way. But our mission hasn't changed: build digital experiences that genuinely move the needle for our clients.

Alex Chen
Co-Founder & CEO

Our Journey

Company Timeline

Milestones that shaped NovaPixel into the agency it is today.

2018

The Beginning

NovaPixel founded by Alex Chen and Maya Rodriguez in a San Francisco co-working space with one client.

2019

First Major Award

Won the Awwwards Site of the Day for our work with a fintech startup. Team grows to 8 people.

2020

Going Remote-First

Transitioned to a fully distributed team across 5 time zones. Shipped 60 projects in a single year.

2021

Mobile Practice Launched

Expanded into native mobile development. Hit 150+ total projects and opened our first physical studio.

2022

AI & Automation Division

Launched our AI automation service line. Partnered with 3 enterprise clients for chatbot and workflow solutions.

2023

Global Expansion

Opened a satellite office in London. Team reaches 35 members across 12 countries. Revenue doubles year-over-year.

2024

250 Projects Milestone

Crossed 250 delivered projects with a 98% client satisfaction rate. Won 4 more design awards. Team grows to 40.
