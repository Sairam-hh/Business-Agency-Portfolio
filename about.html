About the studio

A senior-only agency for teams that value calm delivery.

Founded in 2011, Meridian & Co. is 38 consultants across strategy, product design, and operations — no juniors on your engagement, no bloat in your invoice.

Our story

Built by operators, for operators.

We started as a two-person practice inside a mid-market retailer, embedded with the leadership team through a hard turnaround. When it worked, other founders asked us to do the same for them.

Fourteen years later the model still holds: small teams, senior seniority, clear scopes, and a bias toward finishing.

How we work

Three principles keep the work honest.

Fewer, deeper. We take on a small number of engagements at a time so partners stay close to the work.

Written before shipped. Every recommendation lives in a document our clients can defend without us in the room.

Leave lighter than we arrived. We measure success by how well the team runs after we leave.

Values

What holds the practice together

Craft over volume

Fewer decks, sharper thinking, and a design bar we can actually defend on a printed page.

Clarity as respect

Simple language and short documents are a form of care for the people who read them.

Compounding trust

Most of our clients come from a client. We protect that pipeline by protecting the work.
