FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project with us.

How long does a typical project take?+

Most website projects take 4–8 weeks depending on scope. Mobile apps range from 8–16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase and keep you updated weekly.

What is your pricing model?+

We offer both fixed-price packages and hourly rates. Most clients prefer fixed pricing for clarity. Projects start at $2,500 for landing pages and scale based on complexity and features.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?+

Yes. We offer monthly maintenance plans that include updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and priority support. You can also manage content yourself via our client dashboard.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?+

Absolutely. We adapt to your existing brand system or help you evolve it. Many of our clients come with established brands that need a digital refresh rather than a full rebrand.

Do you offer post-launch support?+

Every project includes 30 days of free post-launch support. After that, you can choose a maintenance plan or contact us on demand. We're always here when you need us.
